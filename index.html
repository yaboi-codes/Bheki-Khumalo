About

A Career at the Intersection of Power & Purpose

Bheki Khumalo is Head of Government Relations at Anglo American South Africa — a role that draws on three decades of executive-level experience spanning presidential communications, multinational corporations, and public sector leadership.

He has served as Spokesperson for President Thabo Mbeki and held communications briefs for several ministers across education, minerals, and energy portfolios — giving him an unrivalled understanding of how government and business intersect.

In the private sector, Bheki has served as Executive Director of Corporate Affairs and Events Management at Siemens SA, and as Head of Corporate Affairs, BEE, and Government Relations at Sasol — where he also sat on the Global Group Executive Committee (GEC).

His board service has spanned the SABC, South African Tourism, the North West Development Corporation (which he chaired from 2007 to 2016), multiple Sasol subsidiaries, and the Black Management Forum. He currently serves on the boards of Anglo American Inyosi and the National Business Initiative (NBI).

Bheki holds a BA, BA Honours, and Higher Diploma in Education from the University of Fort Hare.
